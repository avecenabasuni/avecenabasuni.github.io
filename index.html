Smart Trash Bin Integrated With Automatic Hand Sanitizer

August 2021 — December 2021

A smart trash can integrated with a hand sanitizer that is a device controlled with an Arduino microcontroller to open or close the trash can and press the hand sanitizer automatically using an ultrasonic sensor. The servo motor is used to control the trash lid and press the hand sanitizer, while the buzzer is used as a hand sanitizer alarm. The lid of the trash can opens and closes automatically by detecting human presence at a distance of 30 cm. The servo motor will move and open the lid of the trash can. Then, the buzzer will turn on and the servo motor will press the hand sanitizer so that the hands are clean from dirt.

Potentiostat for Cyclic Voltammetry Analysis using Microcontroller ESP32

July 2022 — January 2023

Potentiostats are electronic instruments used in electrochemical research to study and measure the electrochemical properties of electroactive materials. This project developed a potentiostat prototype with an affordable price (around 537,600.00 IDR) and an open-source system for cyclic voltammetry analysis. The potentiostat prototype is made using commercially available electronic components, such as ESP32 microcontrollers, op-amps, resistors, capacitors, and diodes. The potentiostat prototype has a current range from -430 µA to +410 µA and a sweep voltage range from -1.5 V to 1.5 V.
